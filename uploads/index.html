About Maredin

A Legacy of Trust,
Built on Expertise

Maredin Wealth Advisors was founded in 2005 with a singular mission: to deliver exceptional financial guidance rooted in value investing principles. Originally established as a boutique family office in Miami, Florida, our focus was on maximizing capital appreciation through disciplined, value-driven strategies inspired by the philosophies of Benjamin Graham, Charlie Munger and Warren Buffett.

As we grew, communities with friends, families, and clients formed around a touching truth, too many high-net-worth individuals and entrepreneurs were receiving generic, untapped advice that failed to address their unique circumstances and goals. Furthermore, they were, regardless of amount of family assets, receiving underwhelming personal care and attention. Recognizing this gap, Maredin transitioned from a family office to an Investment Advisor, expanding our services to offer the same level of care, precision, and expertise to a broader audience.

Along the way, we also modified our approach. Drawing inspiration from legendary investor Stanley Druckenmiller, we enhanced our value foundation by incorporating multi-strategy macro-focused investment techniques designed to adapt to evolving markets and unlock greater opportunities. This evolution ensures our clients receive a balance of discipline, innovation, and performance.

A key aspect that sets Maredin apart is our entrepreneurial heritage. Coming from a family of entrepreneurs, this perspective drives our ability to craft holistic strategies that help our clients grow their businesses, preserve their wealth, and build lasting legacies.

Investment Philosophy

Where Discipline
Meets Innovation

Over nearly two decades as wealth advisors and investors, we have developed a proprietary philosophy that combines time-tested value investing with adaptive macro strategies.

We believe in combining a macroeconomic view of the market with fundamental analysis, focusing on broad economic trends while conducting in-depth research on individual investments and their intrinsic value. We aim to identify undervalued opportunities with potential for long-term growth, while prioritizing risk management and position sizing.

Multi-Strategy Macro Approach

Inspired by legendary macro investor Stanley Druckenmiller, we make decisions based on top-down macroeconomic perspectives combined with bottom-up fundamental analysis. We are not limited to any specific investment style or asset class, seeking opportunities across various investments using analysis of market conditions, Fed policy, geopolitics, and sentiment.

Value Investing Foundation

Rooted in the principles of Benjamin Graham, our approach focuses on determining the Intrinsic Value of every investment. We invest only at significant discounts to intrinsic value — typically 40-60% below — ensuring every position carries what Graham called a built-in "margin of safety."

Risk Management & Precision

We prioritize risk management and position sizing to maximize returns while minimizing potential losses. Our disciplined approach balances innovation with proven techniques, adapting to evolving markets while maintaining unwavering commitment to capital preservation.
