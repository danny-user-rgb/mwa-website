Our Story

Where It All Began

Maredin Wealth Advisors was founded in 2005 by Marcelo Zinn, after serving as head Portfolio Manager at Juresa Ltda in Brazil. The firm began as a boutique family office in Miami, focused on disciplined, value-driven strategies inspired by the philosophies of Benjamin Graham, Charlie Munger, and Warren Buffett.

As we grew, a pattern emerged: high-net-worth individuals and entrepreneurs were receiving generic advice that failed to address their unique circumstances. Too often, regardless of family assets, they received underwhelming personal care and attention.

Recognizing this gap, Maredin transitioned from a family office to a Registered Investment Advisor — expanding our services while preserving the same level of care, precision, and expertise. Drawing from both value investing and the multi-strategy macro approach of Stanley Druckenmiller, we craft strategies that adapt to evolving markets and unlock greater opportunities.

Our Commitment

Why Fiduciary Matters

As a fiduciary, we are legally and ethically bound to act in your best interest — at all times, without exception.

Your Interests First

Unlike commission-based advisors, we provide transparent, unbiased financial advice. Our only commitment is to you — not to product manufacturers or institutions.

Full Transparency

We fully disclose all fees, compensation structures, and any potential conflicts of interest. You always know exactly what you're paying and why.

Independent Judgment

As a fully independent firm, no institution dictates our recommendations. Every decision is driven by rigorous analysis and your specific goals.
