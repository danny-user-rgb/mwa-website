About Maredin

Rooted in Miami.
Built on Trust.

Founded in 2005 as a boutique family office, Maredin Wealth Advisors has grown into an independent investment advisor serving high-net-worth individuals, families, and entrepreneurs across Miami and beyond.

As a fiduciary, our loyalty rests solely with our clients free from institutional conflicts of interest. We combine value investing principles with adaptive macro strategies to protect and grow your wealth for generations.
