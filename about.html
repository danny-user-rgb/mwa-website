Our Story

Where It All Began

Maredin Wealth Advisors is a boutique, independent, Registered Investment Advisor based in Miami, Florida. Founded in 2005 by Marcelo Zinn, Maredin is built on the Value Investing philosophy pioneered by Benjamin Graham and popularized by his most famous student, Warren Buffett.

At Maredin, we pride ourselves on delivering financial services and products that help people and firms attain their financial goals. This is achieved through close consultation with our clients, independent research and analysis, and a clear focus on long-term results.

What began as a boutique practice evolved into a comprehensive wealth advisory firm — where every client feels as though they have their own personal "family office." Our fiduciary commitment means we put you first, always.

How We Invest

Separately Managed Accounts

Maredin manages Separately Managed Accounts (SMAs) — individually tailored portfolios based on each client's risk tolerance and goals. Each portfolio shares the same disciplined manager and philosophy, yet is designed for a specific investment objective.

The Goodwin Portfolio

Long-Term Capital Growth

Ideally suited for investors looking for long-term capital growth through disciplined selection of undervalued companies with strong fundamentals.

The Ibis Portfolio

Dividend & Interest Income

Specifically managed to deliver investment income in the form of dividends and interest — ideal for investors seeking consistent, reliable cash flow.

The Cinder Portfolio

Equity Growth via ETFs & Funds

Focused on equity growth sourced from ETFs, Mutual Funds, and other vehicles — a diversified approach to capturing broad market opportunities.

The Fort Portfolio

Income from ETFs, Bonds & Funds

Designed to provide dividend and interest income from ETFs, Mutual Funds, Bonds, and other sources — rather than from individual investment selections.

Account Types We Manage

Across all four portfolios, Maredin manages individual, joint, business, and savings accounts, as well as the full spectrum of retirement accounts — including Traditional IRA, Roth IRA, Rollover IRA, SEP IRA, SIMPLE IRA, and 401(k) plans.

Why Maredin

Built Around You

When Maredin was founded, our primary goal was to provide long-term outperformance to accredited (high net worth) investors. But after numerous conversations with clients, friends, and families about their financial situations and goals, we saw a clear problem: people were simply not getting the financial advice they needed.

They were being told what they wanted to hear — not what was best for them. They were receiving boilerplate recommendations, not tailored solutions. And worst of all, they were not getting the results they deserved.

In response, we evolved our practice into a full wealth advisory firm where every client feels as though they have their own personal "family office." People look to Maredin because we are an independent, boutique Registered Investment Advisor that has earned the trust of its clients by always putting them first — a true fiduciary's responsibility.

At our core, we focus on crafting tailored financial plans, delivering superior results, providing great service, and prospering together. Whether participating in one of our managed portfolios or working with a custom strategy built specifically for you, we know you will love working with us for the long term.

Your Interests First

Unlike commission-based advisors, we provide transparent, unbiased financial advice. Our only commitment is to you — not to product manufacturers or institutions.

Full Transparency

We fully disclose all fees, compensation structures, and any potential conflicts of interest. You always know exactly what you're paying and why.

Independent Judgment

As a fully independent firm, no institution dictates our recommendations. Every decision is driven by rigorous analysis and your specific goals.

Our Journey
