About Maredin

A Legacy of Trust,
Built on Expertise

Maredin Wealth Advisors was founded in 2005 with a singular mission: to deliver exceptional financial guidance rooted in value investing principles. Originally a boutique family office in Miami, we focused on maximizing capital appreciation through disciplined, value-driven strategies inspired by Benjamin Graham, Charlie Munger and Warren Buffett.

As we grew, we recognized a common truth — high-net-worth individuals and entrepreneurs were receiving generic advice that failed to address their unique circumstances. Maredin transitioned to an Investment Advisor, expanding our services to offer the same level of care, precision, and expertise to a broader audience.

As a fiduciary and independent firm, our loyalty rests solely with our clients. Free from institutional conflicts of interest, we are relentless in our commitment to helping you achieve your goals. Drawing inspiration from Stanley Druckenmiller, we enhanced our value foundation with multi-strategy macro techniques — ensuring our clients receive a balance of discipline, innovation, and performance.

Investment Philosophy

Where Discipline
Meets Innovation

Over nearly two decades, we have developed a proprietary philosophy combining time-tested value investing with adaptive macro strategies.

We combine a macroeconomic view of the market with fundamental analysis — focusing on broad economic trends while conducting in-depth research on individual investments and their intrinsic value. We aim to identify undervalued opportunities with potential for long-term growth, while prioritizing risk management and position sizing.

Multi-Strategy Macro Approach

Inspired by Stanley Druckenmiller, we combine top-down macroeconomic perspectives with bottom-up fundamental analysis — seeking opportunities across asset classes using analysis of market conditions, Fed policy, geopolitics, and sentiment.

Value Investing Foundation

Rooted in the principles of Benjamin Graham, we focus on determining the intrinsic value of every investment. We invest only at significant discounts — typically 40–60% below intrinsic value — ensuring every position carries a built-in margin of safety.

Risk Management & Precision

We prioritize risk management and position sizing to maximize returns while minimizing potential losses — balancing innovation with proven techniques and unwavering commitment to capital preservation.
